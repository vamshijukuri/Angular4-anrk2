Welcome to Anurag Engineering College:

Anurag Engineering College is established in 2001 and is reputed as one of the most preferred college for engineering education within short time. The college has created a resourceful and inspiring academic ambience and world class infrastructure. The college is Private Self Financing Institution. The College is approved by AICTE, New Delhi. The College is permanently affiliated to Jawaharlal Nehru Technological University, Hyderabad (JNTUH). TEQIP granted College. An UGC Autonomous Institution.



Nigama Educational Society is established in 2007 with an aim of creating a world class institution for engineering education. The Society comprises of an inspiring team of reputed educationists, academicians, industrialists and entrepreneurs whose valuable knowledge and experience stand as the pillars of support for the institution. The Chairman of the Society Mr. Ch. Devender Reddy is an engineer, who has established more than 40 higher educational institutions in Warangal, Karimnagar and Ranga Reddy districts of Andhra Pradesh. The Secretary and Correspondent Dr. P. Rajeshwar Reddy is a Doctorate in Physics and is an eminent educationist. He is instrumental in establishing more than 10 Colleges offering Engineering, MBA, MCA, Pharmacy, Education and other programmes in Andhra Pradesh.
